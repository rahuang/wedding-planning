Thank you very much for your contact and for your interest in hosting your event at Craveiral Farmhouse.

Craveiral Farmhouse is located in one of the most beautiful regions of Portugal, Alentejo, where nature, countryside and ocean meet. It is a retreat where contemporary comfort and authenticity coexist in harmony, making every moment special and memorable.

More than just a place to celebrate, Craveiral Farmhouse was born from a clear vision: to contribute to the regeneration of the territory through a model based on circular economy principles, the appreciation of local products, collaboration with regional producers and partners, and active integration with the surrounding community.

Sustainability (financial, social and environmental), simplicity, comfort, modernity, freedom, authenticity and integration into local life are our core values. Our mission is to provide our community and each guest with agricultural, gastronomic, hospitality and nature-based experiences, all interconnected, with a strong focus on well-being and mental health. Our vision is to help regenerate the region and establish “our home” as a benchmark for circular economy within the territory.

We embrace the “farm-to-table” concept: the vegetable garden and local produce are part of the property’s daily life and inspire our gastronomy, creating genuine, sustainable and memorable experiences. Our houses, fully equipped with kitchen, utensils, coffee machine and all necessary amenities, allow each stay to be enjoyed with freedom and tranquility, as a natural extension of your own home, yet deeply connected to the surrounding nature.

Each event at Craveiral Farmhouse is designed as a unique and immersive experience, where the setting, flavors and natural surroundings create unforgettable and distinctive moments for everyone present.

For weddings, we operate on an exclusive basis (buyout), ensuring that the couple and their guests have the entire property reserved just for them. More than a single day, the wedding becomes a complete experience, allowing you to celebrate, connect and enjoy every moment with total peace of mind.
