First of all, thank you so much for your interest and welcome to Le Domaine du Grand Lauron.

 

Located in Provence, in the heart of the Luberon, our estate offers an authentic, elegant, and natural setting—perfect for celebrating an unforgettable wedding. Between the natural lake, swimming pool, charming guesthouses, and Mediterranean-style landscaped grounds, we strive to offer you a truly unique and tailor-made experience.

 

We offer the entire estate as a fully private rental, from Friday at 5 p.m. to Monday at 10 a.m., or midweek from Monday at 5 p.m. to Thursday at 10 a.m., so you can fully enjoy every space during your stay—beyond just the big day.

 

The estate includes:

Accommodation for 37 guests across 18 bedrooms (gîtes and guest rooms)
The lakeside area for your ceremony (secular or religious) or cocktail (chairs included in the rental)
All furniture needed for the wedding
An orchard and several terraces for cocktails, brunch, or welcome party
An inner courtyard for outdoor dinners
An indoor reception hall with lake view (seating up to 150 people, with 160 cm round wooden tables and wooden chairs included)
A dance floor overlooking the lake for your evening party until 4 a.m. (inside party only)
A “Bistrot” area and terrace for family moments
Pool area, pétanque court, ping pong table, and swing
 

Chairs for the ceremony, a BOSE sound system, dining furniture, cocktail and brunch furniture, lounge areas, pool house, outdoor kitchens… everything is here and included in the rental to help you create an event that reflects who you are, in a place that feels like you.
