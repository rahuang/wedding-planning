To give you some details on Troutbeck, our unique property is spread over 250 acres in the New York countryside, offering 37 beautiful and modernized guest rooms, suites, cottages, and over 5,000 square feet of indoor event space, beautifully redesigned and renovated. Ever improving, we are delighted to announce the debut of 4 stand-alone cottages, and gatehouse, in Winter 2026.   
 
Troutbeck’s event spaces offer a myriad of possibilities for your wedding weekend. From a Rehearsal Dinner at the Barns, to a seated Reception dinner in our Ballroom, or an intimate Farewell Brunch from our Manor House, we will work with you closely to curate a guest experience that reflects the two of you.  
 
The Ballroom can accommodate up to 200 guests for dinner and dancing, with the opportunity to expand up to 250 guests, tenting on-site. Our wedding weekends are structured as full property buyouts, with exclusive use of all spaces, from Saturday at 12:00pm to Sunday at 12:00pm. 
 
We are delighted to work with a medley of talented vendors, featured on our preferred vendor list here. To note, a wedding planner is required for all weddings on-site.
