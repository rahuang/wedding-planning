Impression

Through this link, you will find our brochure including a room overview and wedding movie to get a good impression what getting married at Borgo Castello Panicaglia looks like.

 

Places to sleep

Our Borgo has 45 places to sleep. This can be increased to a maximum of 51 persons by adding extra beds.

We work together with multiple hotels next door at 5-minute drive which can easily accommodate the remainder of your guests.

 

Number of guests

During a wedding at the Borgo, you rent the whole location exclusively for your wedding. There will be no other guests present.

We have a minimum number of 40 adults (12+). Of course, you can invite less guests, but we always count from 40 adults.

The maximum capacity for a wedding at the Borgo is 75 guests.

 

Full-service weddings

We do only full-service weddings. This includes:

Welcome drink
Pizza party
Breakfast
Lunch
Ceremony toast
Wedding cake
Dinner
Wedding party
Breakfast
Open bar
 

Drinks and catering are exclusively provided by us.

 

Costs location rent 2027

2 nights midweek €15.500,-
2 nights weekend €16.550,-
 

3 nights midweek €23.250,-
3 nights weekend €24.825,-
 

 

The location rent is excluding breakfast.

 

Total costs

You can count on a minimum of 400 euros per person for food and beverage during a 2,5-day wedding. This translates to a total amount of minimum 15.500 – 25.000 euros.

For 40 people, the total (including the 15.500-euro rental) starts from 30.250 euros for a 2,5-day full package wedding.

 

Our rental prices include:

The rental of our standard set of vintage wooden folding chairs with white cushions for ceremony and diner
Wooden dining tables
Wooden ceremony arch
Lamp posts
Vintage 6/7/8-piece cutlery set
1 standard set of white linen
A professional speaker set for the ceremony, pizza evening and dinner (not available as music installation for a DJ/party evening).
 

Payment

We do not organize weddings where guests must pay for drinks, food, and/or accommodation by themselves, as we are not able to organize that in a proper way. All costs go on one bill and need to be paid by the wedding couple.

 

Alternatives in case of rain

If it rains, an outdoor dinner and party can be organized inside under a stretch tent. This is included in the location rental.

 

Availability

From the 17th of July until the 28rd of August 2027, we do not organize any weddings because of the summer holidays.

Supplier services

We also offer supplier services for 3250 euros. We will help you create an itinerary, arrange suppliers, and will make sure everything runs smoothly during the wedding. If you do not have an external wedding planner, we can provide this service.
