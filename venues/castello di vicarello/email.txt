Brief History of Castello di Vicarello

Castello di Vicarello is a 12th-century castle nestled in the rolling hills of Maremma, Tuscany. Once a medieval fortress, it has been lovingly restored by the Baccheschi-Berti family, who transformed it into an intimate and refined retreat where history, elegance, and comfort harmoniously coexist.
Surrounded by vineyards, olive groves, and untouched countryside, Vicarello offers a timeless atmosphere in which past and present meet, the perfect setting for unforgettable celebrations.

Weddings and Events at Castello di Vicarello
At Castello di Vicarello, we specialize in organizing prestigious and luxurious events, including weddings and milestone celebrations such as birthdays and anniversaries, offering impeccable and personalized service.

We are fully equipped to host events of up to 200 guests, with top-tier catering services and everything needed to create an unforgettable experience. Our wedding and event offerings are based on two key components:

Exclusive rental of the castle, required to organize any event.

Minimum Food & Beverage (F&B) spend.

These elements ensure we can deliver an extraordinary, tailor-made experience for each event.
