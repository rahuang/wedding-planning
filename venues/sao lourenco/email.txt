OUR HOTEL & CAPACITIES



Our hotel and Monte Alentejano is a property over 200 years old surrounded by millenary olive trees, wild flowers, vineyards and more than 700 hectares of pure natural landscape. It is in this setting that you can celebrate your wedding, with the harmony of the landscape and the comfort of our service.

 

São Lourenço do Barrocal has the capacity to accommodate up to 114 people, spread over our houses (which have 1 to 3 bedrooms), rooms and suites. In terms of catering, we can accommodate a maximum of 120 guests for meals. The outdoor venues are part of the legacy and identity of São Lourenço do Barrocal, this being our first suggestion for this type of celebration, as our indoor venues are limited. We advise and should be considered, for events with a larger number of guests, the rental and assembly of a tent or outdoor structure.

 

We would like to inform you that we only host weddings when the event takes place on an exclusive basis, meaning you need to book all the rooms of the hotel for a minimum of 2 or 3 nights - as this is the only way we can guarantee that the property will be reserved for you, your family and loved ones.
